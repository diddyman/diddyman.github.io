Larry's Barbershop: About

About Larry's Barbershop

Larry's barbershop was designed to be a neighborhood shop - a modern store built with respect for the community atmosphere of the traditional barbershop. Its an intimate spot where regulars and new-comers can hang out, get a haircut and shave from experienced barbers, discover new and interesting products that may add to their grooming routine or their daily lives, and build long lasting relationships with others in the neighborhood.
